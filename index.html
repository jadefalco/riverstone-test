Taller walls and walls near property lines often require permits. We help determine what is needed and coordinate the required drawings and approvals for your project.

Base depth depends on wall height and soil conditions. We excavate to compacted native soil or structural fill, typically well below the frost line. For excavations deeper than four feet, we coordinate locates with FORTIS BC and BC 1 Call.
