Our Services

Retaining Walls & Outdoor Construction

From structural walls to finished hardscapes, we provide the services your Kamloops property needs.

Structural Solutions

Retaining Walls

We design and build retaining walls that solve slope problems, create usable yard space, and protect your property from erosion. Every wall is built with proper drainage, compacted base material, and the right block or stone for the site.

From small garden walls to engineered tiered systems, we handle the excavation, base preparation, and installation from start to finish. For deeper excavations, we coordinate locates with FORTIS BC and BC 1 Call to keep the site safe.

  • Concrete block and natural stone walls
  • Tiered and engineered systems
  • Drainage and backfill management
Site Prep

Excavation

Our excavation services prepare sites for retaining walls, hardscapes, and landscape transformations. We remove unwanted material, rough grade slopes, and trench for drainage and utilities with precision.

Whether you need a small backyard dig or a larger hillside cut, we choose the right equipment to minimize disruption and complete the work efficiently.

  • Wall and hardscape excavation
  • Drainage and utility trenching
  • Grading and material removal
Built Features

Hardscaping

We build patios, walkways, steps, and seating walls that complement your home and stand up to Kamloops weather. Using quality pavers, natural stone, or concrete, we focus on solid bases, consistent joints, and clean finishing.

Hardscaping adds structure and usability to outdoor spaces while reducing maintenance and improving drainage.

  • Patios, steps, and seating walls
  • Natural stone and paver features
  • Edge restraint and compaction
Finishing

Landscaping

After walls and hardscapes are in place, we finish the surrounding landscape with grading, topsoil, sod, planting beds, and mulch. Our landscaping work blends new construction into your yard for a polished, cohesive look.

We focus on improving drainage, controlling erosion, and creating a finished appearance that ties the whole project together.

  • Grading and topsoil placement
  • Sod and planting bed prep
  • Mulch and edge finishing
Connected Spaces

Patios & Walkways

We install patios and walkways that connect your outdoor spaces and handle everyday use. Each project starts with a compacted base and proper slope for drainage, followed by paver or stone placement and edge restraint.

The result is a level, long-lasting surface that looks great year after year and complements your retaining walls and landscape.

  • Paver and flagstone patios
  • Walkways and garden paths
  • Proper base and drainage slope
Surfaces

Driveways

We construct and repair driveways with a focus on durability and drainage. From gravel bases to paved finishes, we prepare the subgrade, install geotextile and compacted aggregate, and finish the surface to handle vehicle traffic and Kamloops freeze-thaw conditions.

A well-built driveway improves access, protects your investment, and completes the look of your property.

  • New driveway construction
  • Base preparation and compaction
  • Paved and gravel finishes
