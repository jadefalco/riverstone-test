About Us

Experience You Can Build On.

Riverstone Excavating is a professional excavation contractor based in Langley, BC, serving homeowners, builders, developers, and commercial contractors throughout the Fraser Valley.

Our Story

Every Successful Project Starts with Proper Site Preparation.

Whether you're building a new home, developing a commercial property, installing underground services, or preparing land for construction, Riverstone Excavating has the equipment and experience to get the job done right.

We work closely with homeowners, builders, and developers to deliver reliable excavation services while keeping projects on schedule. Our approach is straightforward: show up when promised, communicate clearly, and leave the site ready for the next phase.

Based in Langley, we understand the soil conditions, drainage challenges, and municipal requirements that come with working in the Fraser Valley. That local knowledge helps us anticipate issues before they become costly delays.
