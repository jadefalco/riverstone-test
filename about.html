Our Story

Quality Outdoor Construction for Kamloops Homes.

Kamloops Retaining Wall has been helping local homeowners transform challenging slopes into functional, attractive outdoor spaces since 2006.

Our focus is straightforward: build retaining walls and hardscapes that last, treat every property with care, and keep communication clear from the first visit to the final walkthrough.

From engineered tiered walls to patios, walkways, and landscape finishing, we bring the same attention to detail to every project—whether it is a small residential wall or a complete backyard transformation.
